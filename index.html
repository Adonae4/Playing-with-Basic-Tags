All About Collies

Below you will find a random FAQ about Collies

Breed: Collie
Typically referred to as "Lassie"

The beautiful Collie has two distinct looks:

  1. Full coat (known as the Rough variety)
  2. Short coat (known as the Smooth variety)

Coat Colors
Collies come in three different coat colors:
  • Sable (Brown and White)
  • Tri-Color (Black and white)
  • Blue-merle (Graytones with black and brown highlights)
    •
    Temperment:
  • Moderate
  • Will typically growl at strangers, but is rarely aggressive
  • Can bark a lot if bored
  • Wonderful with children
